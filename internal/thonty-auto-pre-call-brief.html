Build Steps
1
Connect Google Calendar to n8n (OAuth)
Authenticate Google Calendar and grant n8n read access to upcoming events.
2
Build n8n Schedule to Check for Upcoming Calls
Create a scheduled workflow that checks every 15 minutes for calls starting within the next 30 minutes.
3
Build Claude Prompt to Extract Prospect Details
Parse calendar event title, description, and attendees to extract prospect name and company.
4
Connect Perplexity API for Prospect Research
Query Perplexity for website, Google Business Profile, reviews, competitors, and recent news.
5
Build Claude Prompt for Brief Generation
Structure the 1-page brief format: business overview, pain signals, competitor landscape, 3 talking points, objection prep.
6
Connect Slack Delivery with Formatted Brief
Deliver the formatted brief to the #pre-call-briefs Slack channel 30 minutes before the call.

Implementation
Build This
1
Connect GHL form webhook to n8n
Set up a webhook trigger in n8n. Point your GHL form's submission webhook at that URL. It fires the moment a lead submits.
2
Build Claude prompt for personalised first-touch messages
The n8n workflow passes lead data (name, business, form answers) to Claude via API. Claude writes a personalised SMS and email tailored to that specific lead.
3
Connect Twilio for SMS + Instantly for email
n8n takes Claude's output and sends the SMS via Twilio API and the email via Instantly. Both arrive within 90 seconds of form submission.
4
Set up Slack notification for replies
When the lead replies to the SMS or email, n8n catches the reply webhook and posts an alert to Slack so Thonty can jump in personally.
5
Test with 10 dummy form fills
Run 10 test submissions with different business names and form data. Verify SMS delivery, email delivery, personalisation quality, and Slack alerts all work end-to-end.
